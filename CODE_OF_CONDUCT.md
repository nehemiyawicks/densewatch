# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

densewatch is an open project, and we want it to be a welcoming space for
everyone who takes part - in issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other
project channels.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Give and gracefully accept constructive feedback.
- Focus on what is best for the project and its users.
- Show empathy and patience toward other community members.

Harassment, personal attacks, and other unprofessional conduct are not
acceptable and may lead to removal from project spaces.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the
maintainer privately: [@nehemiyawicks](https://github.com/nehemiyawicks).
Reports will be reviewed and handled confidentially.
